K Media
K Media is a social media platform aimed at Connecting Kyambogo University.
Privacy Policy.
Our Privacy Policy helps explain our data practices, including the information we process to provide our Services.
Information we collect.
We must gather some information about you inorder to operate, for example we use your name and profile photo to identify you when you post. We acquire your email and name when you create an account. You can optionally provide your profile and cover photo.
Information you provide.
  • Your name
    You provide a name when creating an account, this could be your real name or a nick name. This information is only displayed on your profile and not shared with any third parties.
  • Your profile and cover photo
    You optionally provide a profile photo. This information is only displayed on your profile and not shared with any third parties.
  • Your email
    You provide an email when creating an account. This information is not displayed on your profile and not shared with any third parties.
  • Your posts
    You post text, photos, audio and videos. Your posts are secured in transit and stored with us until you delete them.
How we use your information.
  • Our services
    We share the information you post with the people you connect with also known as your followers, that is our service.
  • Business Interactions
    We enable you and third parties, like businesses, to communicate and interact with each other using our Services. This is mainly done through advertisements.
Assignment, Change Of Control, And Transfer
In the event that we are involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, bankruptcy, or sale of all or some of our assets, we will share your information with the successor entities or new owners in connection with the transaction in accordance with applicable data protection laws.
Managing
You can delete your personal information from within the app by going to settings, account .. When you delete your account, your information will be also be deleted.
Update to our Privacy policy
We may update our privacy policy, when that happens we will notify you.
